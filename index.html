This R tutorial demonstrates the use of text mining in the context of social research. This workshop is presented by the Center for Population Computing and Methods Core at the Columbia Population Research Center. During this half-day event, faculty will present on the use of text mining in their own research followed by a hands-on tutorial on the processing, analyzing, and visualizing of text-based data in R.

This half-day workshop is supported by Foundations for Research Computing, a collaboration among CUIT, the Office of Research, and Columbia Libraries.
